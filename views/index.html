Saturday 19th July 2014

the day before

Come and join us for a pre-wedding get together on Friday July 18 2014.

This will take place at Doggett's Coat and Badge pub in Blackfriars, in Central London. It will be a great chance to get together and kick start the weekend celebrations.

We’ve booked the top-floor of the pub with its own private terrace overlooking the Thames and we’d love it if you could join us. To twist your arm just a little more a BBQ dinner will be provided!

Doggett's Coat and Badge
1 Blackfriars Bridge,
London
SE1 9UD

Come and join us from 6pm

getting to the church

For guests travelling down from London we have hired a traditional London Routemaster Bus to help people get to the church. The bus will be collecting guests from a central London destination at 12:30pm.

The exact location is still to be finalised, however, the bus will only be permitted to stop for a short time and will therefore require guests to arrive on time, or otherwise have an alternative route.

Please let us know via the RSVP form if you'd like to get the wedding bus to the church

The bus will be making a stop at Sanderstead station at 13:20 should anyone wish to make their own way that far.

Sanderstead is the closest station (Purley Oaks is an alternative), but it is a half hour uphill walk from there. The 403 bus stops outside the station however and runs into Sanderstead village every 12 minutes or so.

For those guests who will be driving there is a car park at All Saints church.

the ceremony

The ceremony will take place at All Saints Church, in Sanderstead at 2pm, Saturday July 19. All Saints is a beautiful church dating back to the 13th century, and is found overlooking the roundabout in Sanderstead village.

getting to the reception

We have hired a traditional red London bus to transport guests from the church to the reception venue.

The journey is a little long, at least an hour, but there will be refreshments provided, and activities to keep you entertained! We (Nick and Dani) are planning to get the bus from the church after the "I do's", and we hope that you'll be able to join us to kick start the celebrations.

We don't anticipate any "comfort" stops however, so some may prefer to make their own way to the reception.

Please let us know on the RSVP form if you'd like to get the wedding bus to the reception

There is also plenty of parking available at Gorse Hill for those that will be driving.

the wedding breakfast

Given our love for all things food and our awareness that choosing a meal is a personal thing we thought it best to leave the choosing of the Wedding Breakfast meal to you!

Simply choose your 3 course meal from the options below

Please make sure that you include your menu choices when replying via the RSVP form, or we may be left to decide on your behalf!

Starter

  1. Grilled goats cheese on all butter pastry wafer with confit red onion and roquet and walnut pesto (v)
  2. Pressed free range chicken terrine wrapped in bacon with pimento and hazelnut vinaigrette
  3. Cod & Atlantic prawn tian topped with creme fraiche and served with Marie Rose dressing crispy leaves and a wedge of lemon

Main

  1. Pot roasted corn fed chicken with champ potato, wild mushroom, garlic and sweet sherry cream
  2. Teriyaki glazed duck breast, on a bed of pommes puree served with a plum and hoi sin sauce
  3. Open ravioli of sun-blushed tomato, baby spinach, basil, pinenuts and cream (v)

Dessert

  1. Classic Beaujolais creme brule with fresh raspberries and lemon shortbread
  2. A shot glass trio of vanilla creme brule, Belgian chocolate truffle and raspberry Eton mess
  3. Classic glazed tart au citron with clotted cream

accommodation

We hope that you have a really enjoyable time at our wedding and that you don’t feel like you need to rush off anywhere. At Gorse Hill there are a number of accommodation options which will fit anyone’s budget. All options include breakfast on the Sunday and so it would be lovely to all be able to relax, and maybe recover, from the previous night over breakfast.

To book your accommodation please contact the reservations team at Gorse Hill directly, either by phone or by email, quoting the name of the wedding party (Nicholas & Danielle), the room type you want, and any additional requirements you have

Tel: (+44) 1483 286500
Email: gorsehill@deverevenues.co.uk
Reservations opening hours are Monday to Friday, 8:00am to 6:00pm

All rooms have en-suite bathrooms, free internet access, a tea & coffee tray, an iron, a hairdryer and a TV.

Mansion House

The Mansion House is the main building in which the reception will take place. The guest rooms are mainly located on the second and third floors, though there are a couple of ground floor rooms too. Each room contains a safe.

£89 per room

The Courtyard

The Courtyard building is just across the 'courtyard' from the Mansion House. The rooms are a little more 'conferency', but easier on the pocket, and a stone's throw from the main building.

£69 per room

The Lodge

The Lodge is a small building about a minute's walk down the driveway from the Mansion House. There are only a few rooms available here, and will be allocated on a first come first served basis!

£49 per room
