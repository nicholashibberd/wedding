Saturday 19th July 2014

the day before

On Friday 18th July we are going to be a pre-wedding get-together in central London, a chance for guests to meet in a relaxed environment ahead of the main event.

This will take place at Doggett's Coat and Badge pub in Blackfriars, on the south side of Blackfriars Bridge, and will kick off from 6pm.

We've booked the top floor, with its own private terrace overlooking the Thames, and we'd love it if you could be there. To twist your arm just a little all food will be provided!

Doggett's Coat and Badge
1 Blackfriars Bridge,
London
SE1 9UD

the church

The ceremony will take place at All Saints Church, in Sanderstead at 2pm. All Saints is a beautiful church dating back to the 13th century, and is found overlooking the roundabout in Sanderstead village.

For guests travelling down from London, our wedding bus will be picking up guests from at 12:30. The bus is only allowed to wait there for a maximum of five minutes, so if you're planning to get this bus, please get there in good time, and have an alternative ready!

Please let us know via the RSVP form if you'd like to get the wedding bus to the church

The bus will be making a stop at Sanderstead station at 13:20 should anyone wish to make their own way that far.

Sanderstead is the closest station (Purley Oaks is an alternative), but it is a half hour uphill walk from there. The 403 bus stops outside the station however and runs into Sanderstead village every 12 minutes or so.

All Saints Church
Onslow Gardens,
Sanderstead
Surrey
CR2 9AB

transport

We have hired a traditional London Routemaster bus to transport guests from the church to the reception venue.

The journey is pretty long, at least an hour, but there will be refreshments provided, and activities to keep you entertained! We (Nick and Dani) are planning to get the bus, and we hope that most of you will too, though we don't anticipate any "comfort" stops, so some may prefer to make their own way to the reception.

Please let us know on the RSVP form if you'd like to get the wedding bus to the reception
